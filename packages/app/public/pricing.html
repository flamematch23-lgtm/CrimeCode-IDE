How payment works

  1. Click any Get <plan> button above — opens @CrimeCodeSub_bot in Telegram.
  2. The bot creates your order and replies with three wallet addresses (BTC / LTC / ETH) and the exact amount to send for each.
  3. You send the exact amount on-chain — the unique last digits act as a discriminator.
  4. The bot detects the transaction (usually 1–15 minutes depending on network) and messages you the license token.
  5. Paste the token into the desktop app → Subscription → I have a license token → Activate. Done.

Team plans

Refunds

We're a crypto-only product, so refunds are handled manually on a case-by-case basis. Contact @OpCrime1312 within 7 days of a monthly/annual order, or within 30 days for lifetime, with your order ID. Lifetime refunds are available only if you haven't activated the token yet.
