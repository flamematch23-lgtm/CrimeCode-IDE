How a new account gets going.

From zero to a working Pro license in under 15 minutes — no support ticket needed.

  1. Open CrimeCode

    Download the desktop binary or open crimecode.cc in a browser. On first launch you see the Subscription gate.

  2. Pick a plan

    Click Pay via Telegram bot on Monthly, Annual, or Lifetime. The bot opens with your order pre-filled.

  3. Send crypto

    The bot replies with three wallets (BTC / LTC / ETH) and the exact amount per wallet. Send from any non-custodial wallet.

  4. Get your token

    Payment confirmed on-chain (~1–15 min) → bot DMs the license token. Paste it into the app's Subscription screen.

  5. Optional: sign in

    Menu → Account → Sign in via Telegram. Enables cloud sync, teams, and device management.

  6. Build a team

    Workspace switcher (bottom-left) → Create Team → invite by handle or email. Role management is built in.
